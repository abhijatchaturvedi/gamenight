📖 How to Play

A social deduction game. One player is secretly the Killer. Can the village find them before it's too late?

🎭 Roles

🔪
Killer
Each night, secretly eliminate one player. Avoid suspicion during the day. Win by reducing players to 2.
💉
Doctor
Each night, secretly protect one player (including yourself). If you save the Killer's target, nobody dies. 1 Doctor per 5 players.
🧑
Villager
You have no special power — but your vote counts. Debate, accuse, and eliminate the Killer to win.

🔄 Game Flow

1
Roles assigned — Each player receives a secret role. Do not reveal it!
2
Night falls — The Killer picks a victim. The Doctor picks someone to protect. Both choose in secret.
3
Dawn — The result is announced: either a player was found dead, or the Doctor saved them.
4
Day discussion — All living players debate and accuse. The dead can only watch.
5
Vote — Everyone votes to eliminate a suspect. Most votes wins. Ties mean no elimination.
6
Reveal — The eliminated player's role is revealed to all. Then night falls again.

🏆 Win Conditions

🏘️Villagers win when the Killer is voted out.
🔪Killer wins when only 2 players remain alive (a tied vote means the Killer kills again).
💡 Tip: Use the Hide Role button if others might see your screen.

The classic game of Xs and Os — now with score tracking and match formats.

🎯 Objective

Get three of your symbols in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — before your opponent does.

🔄 How to Play

1
Two players are randomly assigned ✕ (X) and ○ (O). Others watch as spectators.
2
X always goes first. Take turns clicking empty cells on the 3×3 grid.
3
The first to place three in a row wins the game. Symbols swap for the next game.
4
If all 9 cells fill up with no winner, it's a draw.

⚙️ Match Formats

∞Free Play — play unlimited games, track score indefinitely.
🏆Best of 3 / 5 / 7 — first to win the majority claims the match.
💡 Tip: The room host sets the match format in Settings before the game starts.

One player draws a secret word while everyone else races to guess it. The faster you guess, the more points you earn.

🔄 How to Play

1
Each round, one player becomes the Drawer. They're shown 3 secret words and must choose one.
2
Everyone else sees only _ _ _ _ _ (blanks) and must type their guess in the chat.
3
Correct guesses earn points based on how quickly you answered. Hints appear after 30s and 55s.
4
The Drawer earns points for each player who guesses correctly. Draw clearly!
5
The round ends when time runs out or everyone has guessed. Roles rotate — everyone gets a turn to draw.
6
After all rounds, the highest scorer wins the game.

🎨 Drawing Tools

✏️
Pencil — free-draw. Adjust color and brush size.
🔲
Eraser — erase parts of your drawing.
🪣
Fill — flood-fill an area with your chosen color.
🗑️
Clear — wipe the canvas and start over.
💡 Tip: Once you guess correctly you can't chat — don't spoil it for others!

Classic card-matching game. Be the first to empty your hand and shout UNO when you're down to one card!

🃏 Card Types

🔢
Numbers (0–9)
Play on a matching color or matching number.
⚡
Action Cards
Skip — next player loses turn.
Reverse — reverses play order (2-player: acts as Skip).
+2 — next player draws 2 and loses turn.
🌈
Wild Cards
Wild — change to any color.
Wild +4 — change color AND next player draws 4 and loses turn.

🔄 How to Play

1
Each player is dealt 7 cards. The top card of the deck is flipped to start the discard pile.
2
On your turn, play a card that matches the color or value of the top card. Wild cards can always be played.
3
Can't play? Draw one card from the deck. If the drawn card is playable, you may play it immediately or pass.
4
When you play down to 1 card, the game automatically shouts UNO for you!
5
First player to play all their cards wins the game.

🏆 Win Condition

🃏Play all your cards to win. Other players' remaining cards don't score — it's a pure race.
💡 Tip: Wild +4 can only be played if you have no other matching card — but this isn't enforced digitally.

A 15-question trivia challenge pulled fresh from the internet. Answer fast — speed earns extra points!

📋 Format

1
The game fetches 5 Easy, 5 Medium, and 5 Hard questions at random from a public trivia database.
2
Each question shows 4 options. Tap your answer before the 20-second timer runs out.
3
Correct answers score 500–1000 points based on speed. Wrong or no answer scores 0.
4
After each question, the correct answer is revealed alongside the updated leaderboard.
5
The player with the highest total score after all 15 questions wins.

🏆 Win Condition

🧠Highest score after 15 questions wins. Ties go to whoever was consistently faster across all correct answers.
💡 Tip: Questions are randomised from thousands of categories — expect anything from science to pop culture!
